<p><strong>The RFID Asset Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Type</li><li>Passive RFID Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID Asset Tracking System market is primarily divided into two types: Active RFID and Passive RFID. Active RFID utilizes battery-powered tags that transmit signals autonomously, allowing for real-time tracking over long distances, ideal for high-value assets. In contrast, Passive RFID relies on tags that are powered by the reader's signal, making them more cost-effective and suitable for various applications, although they have limited range and require closer proximity for communication. Both types enhance asset management efficiency across industries.</p></p>
